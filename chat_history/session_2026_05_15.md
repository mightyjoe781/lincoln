### 10. Fix Runtime Errors Post-Deployment
_15:20 – 15:23 IST_

> After bringing the full stack up with `docker compose up --build`, three sequential runtime errors surfaced and were resolved:
>
> 1. **`ModuleNotFoundError: No module named 'email_validator'`** — `EmailStr` in `app/schemas/auth.py` requires the `email-validator` package. Fixed by changing `pydantic>=2.7.0` to `pydantic[email]>=2.7.0` in `pyproject.toml`.
>
> 2. **`ValueError: password cannot be longer than 72 bytes`** on `POST /api/v1/auth/register` — `passlib` is unmaintained and incompatible with `bcrypt>=4.x`. Its internal self-test crashes during backend initialisation. Fixed by removing `passlib[bcrypt]` entirely and calling `bcrypt.hashpw` / `bcrypt.checkpw` directly in `app/core/security.py`.
>
> 3. **Unit tests broken by Celery refactor** — `DocumentService.upload` now returns `status=pending` immediately, but the service tests expected `status=done`. Fixed by adding `tests/unit/conftest.py` with an autouse fixture that mocks `parse_document_task.delay`, and rewriting `test_document_service.py` to assert `status=pending` and verify the task is enqueued with the correct document ID.
